## 🔧 **Common Mistakes to Avoid**

- ❌ Using `=` instead of `==` for comparisons
- ❌ Forgetting quotes around strings
- ❌ Not converting input() to the right type
- ❌ Using unclear variable names
- ❌ Forgetting to print your results
